About Us

AI Powered Learning – Faster, Smarter, More Effective.

Our Mission

SmartEduX is created with one goal: to revolutionize the study experience. It uses artificial intelligence to automatically summarize long lecture videos, extract key points, and organize course notes so students can study faster and more efficiently.

Whether it is university lectures or online tutorials, SmartEduX helps learners understand complex topics in minutes instead of hours, making the learning process simpler, quicker, and more effective.
