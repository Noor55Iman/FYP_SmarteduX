Why Choose SmartEduX?

How does the Video Summarizer work?
Speech-to-text AI technology.
We transcribe audio, detect slide headings and timestamps, extract key concepts and convert them into structured, exam-friendly notes.
Is it personalized?
Adaptive suggestions based on you.
By tracking quiz results and question performance, SmartEduX recommends focused modules and practice sets tailored to your weak areas.
Better than standard E-learning?
Active recall vs Passive watching.
Most platforms just give you videos. We give you the tools to actually retain the information: instant notes and self-testing.
How do MCQs improve retention?
Spaced practice + instant feedback.
Regular low-stakes quizzes help identify gaps, increase recall through spaced repetition and let the AI refine future practice content automatically.
